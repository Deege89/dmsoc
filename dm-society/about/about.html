About the Dungeon Master's Guild

Who & where we are

The DM's guild is a group of experienced Dungeon Masters that will run a game of D&D for you for a fee. At the moment we are based in Melbourne, Victoria but welcome interested Dungeon Masters from all around the world that wish to apply. To see our available Dungeon Masters, click here.

What we provide

  • Someone who knows the rules of Dungeons & Dragons and is experienced in running the game for people
  • All the necessary equipment to play Dungeons & Dragons, like character sheets and dice
  • A published adventure for your group to play, or an adventure tailored specifically for your group.
  • A commitment to providing a high-quality, engaging, fun experience for you and your friends.

What you provide

  • The people
  • The location
  • Any snacks or drinks you want while you play
