Session costs

Your first session, session 0, is completely free. It's a chance for you to meet your DM, get to know each other, let them know what you want, create characters, and learn about the campaign you're about to start.

Every regular session costs $5 per player per hour. Book now to take advantage of our special offer of 50% off for your first 5 sessions!

Once you book a session, one of our DMs will be in touch with you soon to get started.

You can prepay via Paypal or direct deposit, or pay your DM on the day
